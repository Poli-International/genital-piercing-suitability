Genital Piercing Anatomy Suitability

Educational reference covering anatomy requirements, gauge, healing time, material recommendations, and contraindications for 8 genital placement types.

18+ Educational Content This tool is an anatomy and jewellery reference for adults considering or researching genital piercings. All content is clinical and educational. Genital piercings should only be performed by APP-certified piercers with specific genital piercing experience.
Important: Anatomy suitability cannot be assessed remotely. All information here is educational — actual suitability requires in-person examination by a qualified APP-certified piercer. Do not perform or modify genital piercings without professional guidance. If you experience pain, unusual discharge, or signs of infection, seek medical attention promptly.
